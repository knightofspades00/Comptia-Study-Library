📋 Week Assignment — IT Certification Study

Work through your chosen exam at your own pace across the week. Choose the exam that matches where you are — Tech+ is beginner-friendly, A+ / Network+ / Security+ are more advanced.

Day 1 · Monday
📖 Study Guide
Pick your exam below. Open the Study Guide and read through at least 3 domain sections. Write down 5 things you didn't already know.
Day 2 · Tuesday
🃏 Flashcards
Work through all flashcard categories. Use the "Got It / Still Learning" buttons. Aim for 75%+ Got It before moving on.
📸 Screenshot your flashcard score
Day 3 · Wednesday
📚 Vocab + Cheat Sheet
Open the Vocabulary tab. Search for any terms you were unsure of on flashcard day. Then review the Cheat Sheet — use it to quiz yourself.
Day 4 · Thursday
❓ Practice Quiz
Take the full practice quiz. Read each explanation — even if you got it right. Target score: 72% or higher (the real CompTIA passing threshold).
📸 Screenshot your score card to submit
Day 5 · Friday
🔬 Activities + Retake
Complete at least one Activity from the Activities tab. If your Thursday score was below 72%, retake the quiz and submit your best score.
📸 Submit best quiz score if improved
📤 How to Submit: When you finish the quiz on Day 4, a score card will appear on screen with a yellow "📸 Screenshot This" prompt. Take a screenshot showing your name in the browser tab, your score, and the exam name — then upload it to Google Classroom. Your tracker on each exam page saves your progress automatically so you can pick up where you left off each day.
