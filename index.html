📋 Week Assignment — IT Certification Study

Work through your chosen exam at your own pace across the week. Choose the exam that matches where you are — Tech+ is beginner-friendly, A+ / Network+ / Security+ are more advanced.

Day 1 · Monday
📖 Study Guide
Pick your exam below. Open the Study Guide and read through at least 3 domain sections. Write down 5 things you didn't already know.
Day 2 · Tuesday
🃏 Flashcards
Work through all flashcard categories. Use the "Got It / Still Learning" buttons. Aim for 75%+ Got It before moving on.
📸 Screenshot your flashcard score
Day 3 · Wednesday
📚 Vocab + Cheat Sheet
Open the Vocabulary tab. Search for any terms you were unsure of on flashcard day. Then review the Cheat Sheet — use it to quiz yourself.
Day 4 · Thursday
❓ Practice Quiz
Take the full practice quiz. Read each explanation — even if you got it right. Target score: 72% or higher (the real CompTIA passing threshold).
📸 Screenshot your score card to submit
Day 5 · Friday
🔬 Activities + Retake
Complete at least one Activity from the Activities tab. If your Thursday score was below 72%, retake the quiz and submit your best score.
📸 Submit best quiz score if improved
📤 How to Submit: When you finish the quiz on Day 4, a score card will appear with the screenshot keys for your device. Make sure your name in the browser tab, your score, and the exam name are all visible — then upload to Google Classroom. Your progress saves automatically in your browser so you can pick up where you left off each day.
💻 On a Chromebook: Hold Ctrl + the Show Windows key — the one near the top of the keyboard that looks like a small rectangle with two stripes next to it (usually the 6th key from the left, where F5 would be on a regular keyboard). Add Shift to drag-select a region instead of the whole screen.
⚠️ Heads up about progress saving: Your streak, mastery, and quiz scores save in this browser only. If your Chromebook clears browser data when you sign out (some school accounts do this), progress will reset. The site still works fine — you just start each day fresh. Check with your IT department if you want progress to persist.
